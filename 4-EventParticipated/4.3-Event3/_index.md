# Seminar Report: "AI From Scratch"

### Event Objectives

- Introduce the process of building a complete AI system from scratch using AWS services.
- Introduce Amazon SageMaker AI for training and deploying machine learning models.
- Introduce Amazon Bedrock and how to access pre-built foundation models.
- Introduce the concepts of Embedding and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) for controlling AI-generated outputs.

### Speakers

- **Nguyen Tuan Thinh** – DevOps/Cloud Engineer – AWS
- **Nguyen Cong Minh** – DevOps Engineer – AWS
- Experts from **AWS Vietnam**
- Academic Supervisor: **Mr. Mai Hoang Dinh** – Lecturer, IA Department – FPT University Ho Chi Minh City

### Key Topics

#### Building an End-to-End AI System on AWS

- Learned the complete workflow, from data collection and preprocessing to model training, deployment, and monitoring.
- Understood the role of each AWS service within an end-to-end AI pipeline, from raw data to production applications.

#### Speech Recognition on AWS

- Learned how speech-to-text technology converts spoken language into text for natural language processing models.
- Explored real-world applications such as voice chatbots, virtual assistants, and large-scale audio processing.

#### Amazon SageMaker AI

- Learned about the platform for preparing data, training, fine-tuning, evaluating, and deploying machine learning models.
- Understood how SageMaker manages the complete lifecycle of custom AI models.

#### Amazon Bedrock

- Learned how Amazon Bedrock provides unified API access to multiple foundation models from providers such as Anthropic, Meta, and Amazon.
- Understood how Bedrock enables rapid development of generative AI applications without training models from scratch.

#### Embedding

- Learned that embeddings represent text, images, or other data as numerical vectors, enabling semantic understanding and similarity comparison.
- Understood that embeddings serve as the foundation for semantic search, data clustering, and especially the retrieval stage in RAG architectures.
- Learned that Amazon Bedrock provides embedding models that convert textual data into vectors for storage in vector databases.

#### Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)

- Learned that RAG combines information retrieval from private knowledge sources with the text generation capability of Large Language Models (LLMs).
- Understood that RAG helps AI produce more accurate and reliable responses by grounding answers in trusted data sources rather than relying solely on pretrained knowledge.
- Explored how RAG can be combined with guardrails to restrict harmful language and inappropriate content while ensuring responses remain within approved knowledge sources.

### Knowledge Gained

#### Understanding End-to-End AI System Design

- Understood the role of each layer in an AI architecture, including data input (speech and text), foundation models, and output control mechanisms.
- Gained a clear understanding of embeddings and their importance in representing semantic information.
- Learned the architecture of RAG and why it often provides better output control than model fine-tuning in many real-world scenarios.
- Understood how RAG works together with filtering mechanisms and trusted knowledge sources to reduce hallucinations and inappropriate AI-generated content.
- Recognized the importance of AI guardrails in ensuring AI systems operate safely and within predefined boundaries.
- Learned practical experiences from AWS Vietnam engineers who shared real-world AI deployment projects on AWS.
- Realized that designing a controllable AI system from the beginning is just as important as improving model accuracy.

#### Networking and Discussion

- The Q&A session provided an opportunity to interact directly with AWS engineers and discuss practical challenges in deploying AI systems.
- Through the discussions, gained valuable insights into the difficulties of moving AI applications from prototype to production environments.

#### Key Takeaways

- A complete AI system consists not only of AI models but also data processing, information retrieval, and output control components.
- Embedding and RAG are fundamental techniques that improve both the accuracy and safety of AI systems when working with private organizational data.
- Choosing between training custom models with Amazon SageMaker and using foundation models through Amazon Bedrock should depend on the specific use case rather than applying a one-size-fits-all approach.
